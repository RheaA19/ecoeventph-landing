The Problem

Students miss eco-events they actually want to join

Environmental awareness is growing among Filipino youth — but translating that awareness into real action has a big friction point.

🔍

Many students are interested in environmental action, but participation remains low due to poorly promoted and scattered environmental events across different social media pages — making them hard to find and easy to miss.

Why Now

Three forces creating a perfect opportunity

The timing has never been better to build the centralized eco-event hub that campus communities need.

01

Rising Awareness

Growing environmental concern among Filipino youth creates a ready and motivated user base eager to participate.

02

Institutional Need

Schools and LGUs require participation data and engagement tools — EcoEventPH delivers exactly that through its analytics dashboard.

03

Market Gap

No centralized eco-event platform currently exists in the Philippines. EcoEventPH is the first-mover with a clear competitive advantage.

Our Solution

One platform. All the campus eco-events.

EcoEventPH removes the friction between environmental intention and environmental action.

📋

Centralized Events

One platform listing every campus environmental event — no more scattered posts across multiple pages and groups.

🎓

Student Features

📊

Organizer Tools
